About us

Aiming to improving the quality of education provided in the rural areas in order to secure the future of the next generation and more generations to come. Efforts by NGOs and community engagement are crucial in addressing these issues, alongside the integration of technology and international partnerships for funding and expertise. A comprehensive approach involving investment in infrastructure, improving teacher training, and policy reforms is essential for achieving inclusive and quality education for every child in Uganda.

who we are

EduAll is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education to children in rural Uganda. Founded in 2020, we've been working tirelessly to bridge the educational gap between urban and rural areas.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every child in Uganda, regardless of their location or economic background, has access to quality education. We believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and creating sustainable development.
